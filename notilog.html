Notilog Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 14, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Notilog. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how Notilog collects, uses, and safeguards your information when you use our mobile application. By using Notilog, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Notilog is designed as a utility to provide you with a comprehensive history of your device's notifications, helping you recover messages you might have missed or dismissed accidentally.

2. Information We Collect

To provide its core functionality, Notilog requires specific permissions to access and store information. These include:

  • Notification Content: We capture and store the titles, text messages, and sender information of notifications that appear on your device. This allows you to view a historical log of your activity.
  • App Metadata: We record the name and icon of the application that generated each notification to help you categorize and search your history.
  • Timestamps: We record the exact time and date each notification was received to provide an accurate chronological timeline.
  • Device Information: We may collect basic device data such as your hardware model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers to ensure the app runs smoothly and to troubleshoot technical issues.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the data collected strictly for the following purposes:

  • Core Service Deployment: To display your notification history, enable search functionality, and allow you to filter logs by app or category.
  • App Personalization: To remember your preferences, such as search history and theme settings.
  • Performance Monitoring: We use aggregated, non-identifiable data to monitor app stability and fix bugs to improve your experience.

4. Data Storage & Local Processing

Security through Locality: One of Notilog's core privacy features is that your notification data is stored locally on your device. Unlike many other apps, Notilog does not upload your private notification content to our servers.

  • Private Sandbox: Your data is stored within the application's private "sandbox," which prevents other apps on your device from accessing Notilog's database.
  • Encryption: While data is local, we recommend using device-level encryption (like a PIN lock) to prevent unauthorized physical access to your history.

5. Advertising & Third-Party Services

To keep Notilog free for everyone, we partner with third-party service providers who may display advertisements or provide analytical services:

  • Google AdMob: We use Google AdMob to serve advertisements. AdMob may use your device's Advertising ID and cookies to serve personalized ads based on your interests and to analyze ad performance.

You can manage your ad preferences or opt-out of personalized ads via your Google Account settings or your device's privacy menu.

6. Your Rights & Data Control

You have full control over your data within Notilog:

  • Deletion: You can delete specific notification entries at any time. You also have a "Clear All" option to wipe the entire database.
  • Revocation: You can revoke Notilog's access to your notifications via your device's System Settings at any time, which will stop all new data collection.
  • Export: If supported by your version, you can export your data for your own records before clearing it from the app.

7. Children's Privacy

Notilog is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal data, we will take steps to delete such information immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top. We recommend you review this policy periodically for any changes.
