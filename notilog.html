Notilog Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 14, 2026

Data Collection

Notilog requires notification access to function. We collect:

  • Content of incoming notifications (sender, app name, and text).
  • Timestamp of notifications received.
  • Application names that generate notifications.

Crucially: All notification logs are stored locally on your device unless you manually export them. We never upload your notification content to our servers.

Third Party Services

Notilog uses minimal third-party integrations:

  • Crashlytics: To monitor app performance and fix bugs. No notification data is sent to Crashlytics.
  • Local Backup: You may choose to back up data to your personal cloud storage (e.g., Dropbox) which is governed by their respective privacy policies.

Security

Your notification history can contain sensitive information. We recommend using the built-in app lock feature (PIN or Biometrics) to secure the app. Data stored locally is sandboxed within the application's private storage.
