Attend Plus Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 14, 2026

Data Collection

Attend Plus helps you track your attendance with ease. To provide this service, we collect:

  • Subject names and schedule timings.
  • Attendance records (Present, Absent, Late).
  • Optional location data if you enable "Geofenced Attendance".

Location data is used only for verifying attendance at a specific coordinate and is never stored on our servers.

Third Party Services

We work with the following partners:

  • Mapbox: For displaying maps and managing geofencing features if enabled.
  • Stripe: If you purchase a premium subscription, payment data is handled securely by Stripe.

Security

We use end-to-end encryption for all data synced with our cloud servers. Only authorized users with the primary account credentials can access attendance logs and reports.
