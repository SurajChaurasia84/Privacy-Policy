TaskManager Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 14, 2026

Data Collection

TaskManager collects minimal data necessary to provide its services. This includes:

  • Task titles and descriptions you enter.
  • Due dates and reminder settings.
  • Device information to sync tasks across your devices.

We do not collect personal identifiers unless you choose to create a cloud backup account.

Third Party Services

We use the following third-party services to enhance your experience:

  • Google Drive: If you enable cloud sync, we use Google Drive to store your encrypted task database.
  • Firebase Analytics: To help us understand how users interact with the app and improve features.

Security

We take the security of your tasks seriously. All data synced to the cloud is encrypted locally on your device before transmission. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect against unauthorized access or disclosure of your data.
