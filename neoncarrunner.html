Neon Car Runner Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 21, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Neon Car Runner. We are dedicated to providing an exciting, fast-paced racing experience while being fully transparent about how we handle any information related to your use of our mobile game. Your privacy is a priority, and this policy outlines our commitment to data security.

By playing Neon Car Runner, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Neon Car Runner is designed to keep your gaming experience private. We collect the following types of information:

  • Game Progress: We store your total coins, high scores, and unlocked features. This data is handled with a focus on local storage.
  • Device Metadata: We may collect basic information about your device, such as the model, OS version, and hardware specifications, to optimize game performance and troubleshoot crashes.
  • Advertising Identifiers: We use industry-standard identifies (like Google Advertising ID) to provide rewarded ad features and analyze ad performance.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely to enhance your gameplay:

  • Persistence: To save your progress so you don't lose your coins or high scores when you close the app.
  • Rewards: To facilitate the "Rewarded Ad" system, allowing you to earn coins or continue your run by watching a video.
  • Optimization: To identify technical bugs and ensure the game runs smoothly at 60fps on a wide range of devices.

4. Data Storage & Locality

Privacy by Design: One of our core philosophies is that your game progress belongs to you. Neon Car Runner stores your primary game data locally on your device.

  • No Cloud Sync: We do not currently upload your private gameplay scores or coin counts to our own servers.
  • Local Buffering: All high scores and currency amounts are written to your device's secure storage area, preventing unauthorized access from other applications.

5. Advertising & Third-Party Services

To provide high-quality content for free, Neon Car Runner works with trusted advertising partners:

  • Unity Ads: We use Unity Ads to deliver rewarded video and interstitial advertisements. Unity Ads may collect information about your device, location, and interaction with ads to provide relevant content and prevent fraudulent activity. You can learn more about their practices in the Unity Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play Services: We use standard Android services for basic app stability and analytics.

6. Your Rights

Since we store data locally, you have direct control over your privacy:

  • Data Removal: You can clear all game data by clearing the app's cache/storage in your device settings or by uninstalling the application.
  • Ad Choices: You can reset or opt-out of personalized advertisements via your Android device's "Privacy" or "Google" settings menu.

7. Children's Privacy

Neon Car Runner is built for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so that we can take the necessary actions.
